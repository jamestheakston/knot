How a pod works

Three steps, then it runs itself.

No streak freezes, no badges to buy. Just a small group and a habit you all agreed to keep.

01

Start a pod

Name the habit and set the daily bar — 500 words, a workout, no takeout, whatever you're actually trying to keep.

02

Bring your people

Share an invite code with 3 to 5 people. Small enough that everyone notices who's missing.

03

Check in, daily

One tap when you've done it. The pod sees the day's board — who showed up, and who didn't, in real time.

Why a pod, not an app

The accountability was always the people.

Knot just gives the group a shared board to keep each other honest.

One shared streak

The pod's streak breaks the day anyone in the group misses — it's a group's record, not five separate ones.

Capped at five

Pods max out at five people on purpose. Big enough to cover a slip, small enough that no one hides in the crowd.

The board, not a lecture

No push notifications shaming you. Just a check-in grid the pod can glance at — you decide what to do with it.
